# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Book

Welcome to the comprehensive guide to **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics**. This book covers the essential concepts, tools, and practical implementations needed to develop intelligent humanoid robotic systems.

## About This Book

This book is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of Physical AI and its application to humanoid robotics. Through a series of structured modules, you'll learn about the core technologies that enable robots to perceive, reason, and act in physical environments.

## What You'll Learn

In this book, you will explore:

- **ROS 2 (Robot Operating System 2)**: The foundation for robotic communication and coordination
- **Gazebo/Unity**: Simulation environments for testing and development
- **NVIDIA Isaac**: Advanced robotics platforms for AI-powered systems
- **Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Models**: How robots integrate perception, reasoning, and action
- **Integration Strategies**: Bringing all components together for complete humanoid systems

## Getting Started

Select a module from the sidebar to begin your journey into Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. Each module builds upon the previous one, providing both theoretical knowledge and practical implementation guidance.
